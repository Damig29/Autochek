Frequently asked questions

What is an Autochek Cash Loan?
Autochek cash loans are online cash loans that provide quick financial assistance to business owners and salary earners, allowing you to use your car as collateral to get access to quick cash.
How do I get a Cash Loan from Autochek?
If you need a loan immediately, Autochek offers quick cash loans in Nigeria. To request a loan, follow these steps:
  1. Visit the Autochek website here.
  2. Fill in your details in the mini form, and proceed to apply for an online cash loan by filling out the rest of the form, then submit.
  3. If you meet the credit requirements, an offer will be sent to your email instantly.
  4. After reviewing the conditional offer, upload the necessary documents.
  5. You will be scheduled for a vehicle inspection.
  6. You will then receive the final offer and your quick cash loan Journey begins.
What documents are needed for application?
  • A government approved ID card
  • A 6 months bank statement
  • Proof of vehicle ownership (Vehicle year of manufacture must be 2010 or newer)
  • Business registration documents (Business owners)
  • Employment letter (Salary earners)
  • Pay slip for the last 3 months (salary earners)
  • BVN
  • Utility bill
  • Comprehensive Insurance ( If your vehicle is not comprehensively insured, Autochek can facilitate this for you)
What is the maximum loan amount available?
The maximum loan amount available to customers is 50 million naira, while the minimum loan amount is 2 million naira.
How long does the approval process take?
It takes about 24 hours or less.
Can I still use my car while servicing the loan?
Yes, you still get to drive your car while repaying the loan.
Can I terminate the loan at my time?
Yes, your loan application can be terminated at any time with no additional charges.
